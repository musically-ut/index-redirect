Redirect via index.html

Get index.html to re-direct.

If you ever have wanted to redirect to a different page, but didn't have control over the web-server, then you you have come to the right place.

Here you can generate a comprehensive index.html that you can download and keep on your site's root to redirect to the desired page. The index.html is modelled after this Stack Overflow answer.
